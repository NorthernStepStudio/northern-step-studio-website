# Product Anchor

## What we are building

- PasoScore mobile-first app (Android + iOS)
- Multilingual UX (Spanish, English, Italian)
- Dual onboarding (Anonymous and Personalized)
- Deterministic credit guidance system
- Subscription model with clear month-by-month value

## What we are not building in v0

- AI credit prediction engines
- Bureau/API integrations
- Risk scoring infrastructure
- Large backend orchestration
- ResponseOS integration

## Build philosophy

- Keep logic deterministic
- Keep UX clear and low-friction
- Keep compliance risk low
- Ship monthly guidance value, not technical complexity

## Privacy boundary

- This app does not access your credit report.
- We do not ask for SSN.
- All data stays on user mobile.

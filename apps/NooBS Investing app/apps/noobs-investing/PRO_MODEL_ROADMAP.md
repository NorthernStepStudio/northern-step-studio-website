# NooBS Investing: Pro Subscription & Roadmap

This document outlines the refined "No-BS" monetization strategy and future development path for the NooBS Investing app.

## 1. Pro Subscription Model (Approved)

The NooBS Pro model is designed to separate basic "emergency stability" education from "advanced execution" and premium simulator features.

### Tiers
- **Basic (Free)**: Access to Lessons 1-10, Paper & Real trading simulation, standard asset market.
- **Pro (Subscription)**: Unlock all Advanced features and Lessons 11-20.

### Pricing (Revenue Generation)
- **Discipline Subscription**: $4.99 / month
  *   *Target*: Casual users wanting ongoing advanced content and smart notifications.
- **Diamond Hands Lifetime**: $29.99 / one-time
  *   *Target*: Long-term believers in the "No-BS" philosophy.

## 4. Key Philosophies for Monetization
1. **Never Sell the Dream**: We don't promise 10x returns. We charge for the tools that keep you disciplined.
2. **Brutally Honest Branding**: Pro isn't "VIP" or "Gold." It's for users who admit they need the guardrails.
3. **Transparent Friction**: The app itself should stay fast and lightweight, unlike the legacy banks we criticize.

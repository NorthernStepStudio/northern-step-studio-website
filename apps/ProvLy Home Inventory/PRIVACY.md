# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: March 4, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how **Northern Step Studio** ("Company", "we", "us") handles data for ProvLy.

## 1. Local-First Model
ProvLy is designed as a local-first app:
- inventory records are primarily stored on your device,
- by default, we do not maintain a central copy of your full inventory.

## 2. Data We May Process
Depending on features you enable, we may process:
- account identity data (email/user ID),
- subscription metadata (plan/entitlements),
- sync/storage metadata,
- diagnostic crash and reliability signals.

We do not sell your personal data.

## 3. Optional Cloud Features
If you enable account and cloud features, trusted processors may be used:
- Supabase (authentication/storage),
- RevenueCat (subscription management),
- app store billing providers (Apple/Google).

These services process data only to provide requested features.

## 4. Data Control and User Rights
You can:
- export inventory data,
- delete account-linked data,
- disable cloud features and keep local-only workflows.

You are responsible for maintaining your own backup strategy where required.

## 5. Security
We use reasonable technical and organizational controls for security of account-linked services. No system is fully risk-free.

## 6. Children's Privacy
ProvLy is not directed to children under 13. Do not use the app if prohibited by local child privacy laws.

## 7. Policy Updates
We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be reflected by the updated effective date.

# Plumbing AI Starter Demo Script

## Opening

Say:

`Let me show you what happens when you miss a call.`

Then:

`If you miss a call, that customer is usually calling the next plumber. This makes sure you respond instantly, even if you are on a job.`

## What It Is

Say:

`This is a 24/7 missed-call response system that captures the lead, qualifies the problem, checks urgency, and sends you a clean summary so you do not lose the job.`

## Live Demo Flow

System:

`Hey, this is Mike from ABC Plumbing. Sorry we missed your call.`

`What's going on - leak, clog, water heater, or something else?`

Customer:

`Leak under the sink`

System:

`Got it. Is the leak constant or only when you use the sink?`

Customer:

`Constant`

System:

`Is this causing flooding or urgent damage right now? Reply yes or no.`

Customer:

`Yes, a little`

System:

`Where is the issue located? Kitchen, bathroom, basement, or somewhere else?`

Customer:

`Kitchen`

System:

`Can I get your name so the technician can reach you?`

Customer:

`John`

System:

`Thanks, John. We've got your request.`

`A technician will reach out shortly to schedule.`

## Reveal

Show the summary and say:

`This is what you receive instantly instead of losing the job.`

## Value Statement

Say:

`It gives you the issue, urgency, location, and customer details right away so you can call back fast and focus on real jobs first.`

## Objections

If they ask:

`What if it says something wrong?`

Say:

`It follows a controlled plumbing script and rules, so it stays on topic.`

If they ask:

`Can I still talk to the customer?`

Say:

`Yes. This just handles the first response so you do not lose the lead.`

If they ask:

`Is this complicated?`

Say:

`No. Once it is set up, it runs automatically.`

## Close

Say:

`If this saves just one missed job per week, it pays for itself.`

Then:

`Want me to set up a demo version for your business?`

## Full Description
**Build smarter, not harder.** NexusBuild is the ultimate AI-powered assistant for PC enthusiasts, gamers, and creators. Whether you're building your first rig or upgrading a beast, NexusBuild makes it easy.

**🤖 AI-Powered Recommendations**
Not sure where to start? Just tell customizable Nexus AI your budget and goal (e.g., "Gaming PC for $1500" or "Workstation for video editing"), and get a complete, balanced parts list instantly.

**✅ Instant Compatibility Checking**
Never worry about incompatible parts again. Our smart builder automatically checks:
*   CPU & Motherboard socket match
*   Case & GPU clearance
*   PSU wattage requirements
*   RAM compatibility
*   And more!

**💰 Price Tracking & Deals**
Save money with real-time price tracking and alerts. See price history charts to know if you're getting a good deal, and browse daily trending discounts on GPUs, CPUs, and more.

**🔎 Advanced Part Search**
Explore a massive database of current and legacy components (2010-2024). Filter by specs, price, and performance to find exactly what you need.

**Features:**
*   AI Chat Assistant for instant advice
*   Visual PC Builder
*   Save & Share multiple builds
*   Benchmark estimates for gaming and productivity
*   Community Build Gallery
*   Dark Mode interface

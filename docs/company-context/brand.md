# NStep Brand Identity

Northern Step Studio (NStep) is a boutique engineering and design studio focused on building high-fidelity, user-centric software and games.

## Core Values
- **Craftsmanship**: We build with precision and intention.
- **Utility**: Every feature must serve a purpose.
- **Aesthetics**: Premium, modern, and high-contrast designs.
- **AI-Native**: We leverage AI to accelerate development and operation.

## Key Products
- **NStep AI**: The broader AI ecosystem.
- **Matterhorn**: The primary AI agent and operator.
- **Synox**: The core intelligence engine.
- **NexusBuild**: Next-gen development and build orchestration.
- **NeuroMoves**: Pediatric motor therapy and education games.
- **ProvLy**: Professional service automation and CRM.
- **Doomed**: A high-fidelity, tactical roguelike RPG.

## Voice and Tone
- **Professional but Modern**: Use concise, impact-driven language.
- **Technical Excellence**: Demonstrate deep understanding of the stack.
- **Action-Oriented**: Focus on solutions and forward movement.

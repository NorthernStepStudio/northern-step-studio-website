# NStep Application Catalog

## 1. NexusBuild
A cross-platform build orchestration system. Handles Android builds, Gradle management, and automated release pipelines.

## 2. NeuroMoves
A suite of educational and therapeutic games for pediatric patients. Focuses on motor skills, letter/number recognition, and cognitive development.

## 3. ProvLy
Enterprise-grade CRM and professional service platform. Manages leads, client relationships, and project billing.

## 4. Doomed (Roguelike)
A tactical, high-fidelity roguelike RPG with a focus on deep combat mechanics and progression.

## 5. NStep Build Center
The internal command center for managing application builds, credentials, and deployment artifacts.

## 6. NStep AI (Matterhorn / Synox)
Matterhorn is the primary AI agent/operator powered by the Synox intelligence engine. It manages project intelligence, repo snapshots, and operational memory.

## 7. Studio Intelligence
The private admin dashboard surface for managing NStep operations, projects, analytics, repo snapshots, and NStep AI modes.

# NooBS Investing Knowledge

This lane is the canonical source for the education-only investing assistant, simulator behavior, and risk explanations.

## What this lane must answer

- What does the simulator teach?
- Why does the assistant refuse personalized advice?
- How does risk tolerance map to scenarios?
- What assumptions does the simulator use?

## MVP truth set

- Learning path and glossary.
- Simulation rules and assumptions.
- Risk explanation docs.
- Education-only boundary.
- Common pitfalls and misconceptions.

## P0 missing items

- Education-only boundary.
- Simulation assumptions.
- Glossary and learning path.

## P1 missing items

- Risk education module.
- Misconception FAQ.
- Scenario-based examples.

## P2 later items

- Structured curricula.

- Experimentation notes for retrieval quality.

## Retrieval hooks

- Questions asking what to buy should trigger a refusal or a boundary answer.
- Questions about diversification, risk, and assumptions should be answered from these docs only.

## Safety notes

- Never provide personalized investment advice.
- Do not imply the simulator predicts market outcomes.
- Keep the education boundary explicit in every answer.
